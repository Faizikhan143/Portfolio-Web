TextUtils React-App

Textutils is a powerful and versatile React application designed to provide a suite of text manipulation tools. This application is ideal for individuals who frequently work with text, including writers, students, developers, and professionals. Textutils enhances productivity by simplifying common text-related tasks and providing instant, accurate results..

Features:

    Text Transformation:

  • 1. Real-time Updates: See changes to your text in real-time as you apply different operations.
  • 2. Responsive Design: The application works seamlessly on various devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
  • 3. Dark Mode: Switch between light and dark themes for a comfortable user experience in different lighting conditions.

    Text Analysis:

  • 1. Word Count: Calculates the number of words in a text string.
  • 2. Character Count: Determines the total number of characters in a text string, optionally including or excluding spaces.
  • 3. Read Time Estimate: Estimates the time required to read the text based on the average reading speed.

    Text Manipulation:

  • 1. Remove Extra Spaces: Eliminates unnecessary spaces between words.
  • 2. Copy to Clipboard: Copies the processed text to the clipboard with a single click.
  • 3. Clear Text: Clears the input text area to start afresh.

NewsMonkey React-App

NewsMonkey is an innovative React application designed to provide users with up-to-date news articles from various sources. It serves as a centralized platform for accessing diverse news content conveniently. With NewsMonkey, users can stay informed about current events, trending topics, and breaking news across different categories and regions.

Features:

    News Aggregation:

  • 1. Multiple Sources: Integrates news articles from a wide range of sources, including reputable publishers and news outlets.
  • 2. Categorized Content: Organizes news articles into categories such as politics, sports, technology, and more for easy navigation.
  • 3. Personalized Feeds: Allows users to customize their news feeds based on their interests and preferences.

    Responsive Design:

  • 1. Mobile Compatibility: Designed to be responsive, ensuring seamless access to news content on smartphones and tablets.
  • 2. Adaptive Layout: Adapts to different screen sizes and orientations, optimizing the user experience across devices.

    Technology Stack::

  • 1. Frontend: React.js for building a dynamic and interactive user interface.
  • 2. Styling: CSS3, Bootstrap for responsive design and modern aesthetics.
  • 3. API Integration: Utilizes news APIs (such as NewsAPI) to fetch and display news content dynamically.

MyOnlineMeal Website

MyOnlineMeal is a website dedicated to offering online food delivery services in Karachi. It provides a platform for customers to order their favorite meals conveniently from the comfort of their homes.

Overview:

  • 1. Navigation: The navigation bar at the top provides easy access to different sections of the website, including Home, About Us, Services, and Contact Us.
  • 2. Home Section: The home section welcomes visitors with a title and introductory text, inviting them to explore the website further. A prominent "Order Now" button encourages immediate action.
  • 3. Services Section: The services section highlights the key offerings of MyOnlineMeal, including food ordering, catering, and bulk ordering. Each service is accompanied by a brief description and an illustrative image.
  • 4. Clients Section: The clients section showcases some of the notable clients or partners of MyOnlineMeal, adding credibility to the brand.
  • 5. Contact Section: The contact section provides a form for visitors to get in touch with MyOnlineMeal. It includes fields for name, email, phone number, and a message box for inquiries or feedback.
  • 6. Footer: The footer contains copyright information and serves as a consistent element across all pages of the website.

Technologies Used:

  • 1. HTML: Markup language for structuring the content of the website.
  • 2. CSS: Styling language used to enhance the visual presentation and layout of the website.
  • 3. Google Fonts: External font library utilized to improve typography aesthetics.

  • 1. Play/Pause: Users can click on the play/pause button to toggle between playing and pausing the currently selected song.
